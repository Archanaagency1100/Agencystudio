Services

With over 28 years of passion and

dedication, Agencyeleven100

Creative Direction

At Agency Eleven100, our Creative Direction service begins with Imagine, where we envision your brand’s story and visual identity. During the Thinking Process, we refine ideas to align with your objectives. In the Planning phase, we develop a strategic roadmap, collaborating with creatives to bring your brand’s vision to life.

Brand Management

We provide brand management services that strategically develop, position, and maintain your brand’s identity to maximize its market impact and value.

Image Consultant

Our image consulting process begins with Assessment, where we evaluate your personal or professional image through consultations, style analysis, and understanding your goals, preferences, and lifestyle needs. In Strategic Planning, we develop a personalized plan with wardrobe updates, grooming tips, and style guidance tailored to your objectives. During Implementation & Coaching, we support you in making these changes, providing shopping assistance, coaching, and ongoing guidance to enhance your appearance and confidence.

Film Production

The film production process at Agency Eleven100 begins with Pre-Production, where we handle planning and preparation, including scriptwriting, casting, location scouting, and budgeting. We create detailed storyboards, production schedules, and assemble the crew to ensure a clear roadmap. During Production, we bring the vision to life by managing all aspects of filming, including directing, camera work, sound, and lighting. In Post-Production, we edit footage, add visual effects, sound design, music, and color grading, transforming the project into a polished final product ready for distribution.

Photography

At Agency Eleven100, our photography process begins with Planning & Concept Development, where we explore your vision, create mood boards, and organize locations, props, and equipment. During Photoshoot Execution, our team directs the shoot, managing lighting, angles, and composition to capture the ideal shots. Finally, in Post-Production & Editing, we enhance selected images through retouching and color correction, delivering polished, visually stunning photos that align with your brand’s style.

Web Development

At Agency Eleven100, our web development process begins with Planning & Strategy, where we assess your goals, target audience, and project requirements, creating wireframes and a strategic plan for a user-friendly website. In Design & Development, we craft visually appealing, responsive designs and develop functional features for optimal performance. Finally, in Testing & Launch, we thoroughly test for functionality, speed, and security, addressing issues before launch and providing ongoing support and maintenance.

UI UX

UX/UI design process begins with Research & Analysis, where we study user needs, business goals, and market trends through research and competitor analysis. In Design & Prototyping, we develop wireframes, user flows, and prototypes to create an engaging, user-friendly experience. Finally, in Testing & Refinement, we conduct usability tests, gather feedback, and make adjustments to ensure the design is intuitive, visually appealing, and aligned with user expectations.

Graphic Design

At Agency Eleven100, our graphic design process begins with Discovery & Concept Development, where we explore your brand’s goals and audience, brainstorming and sketching ideas to develop a fitting concept. In Design & Creation, we transform the concept into detailed designs using typography, color, and imagery to effectively communicate your message. Finally, in Feedback & Refinement, we incorporate your feedback, refining the design to ensure it meets your expectations and resonates with your audience.

Art Direction

At Agency Eleven100, our art direction process starts with Research & Inspiration, where we analyze your brand, target audience, and project goals to set the creative tone. In Conceptualization & Design Guidance, we develop visual concepts, mood boards, and style guides to direct the design process. Finally, in Team Collaboration & Quality Control, we work with designers and creatives, providing feedback and ensuring that every element meets high standards of quality and consistency.
